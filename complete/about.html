    Our Mission

    Why CodingBooks exists

    Learn by doing

    Every lesson comes with an interactive code editor so you can experiment, run code, and see results instantly — not just read theory.

    Structured paths

    Follow clear, beginner-friendly learning paths that guide you step-by-step from your first HTML page to building full responsive websites.

    Free for everyone

    Quality education should not be locked behind a paywall. CodingBooks will always be free to use for any learner around the world.

    Our Story

    Built by learners, for learners

    CodingBooks started as a simple idea: make web development education as clear and approachable as possible. Too many beginners get stuck on outdated tutorials, messy examples, or content that's hidden behind subscriptions.

    We built CodingBooks to fix that. Every page is designed to be short, visual, and immediately practical — so you spend your time learning, not searching.

    Whether you're writing your very first <h1> tag or building your tenth project, we're here to help you grow into a confident web developer.
