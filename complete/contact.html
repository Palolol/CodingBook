    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is CodingBooks really free?
    Yes! CodingBooks is and will always be completely free for learners around the world. We believe quality education should be accessible to everyone.
    Do I need any prior coding experience?
    Not at all. Our lessons start from the very basics, assuming no prior knowledge. Each topic builds on the previous one in a clear, friendly order.
    How do I track my learning progress?
    After creating a free account, your lesson completions are automatically saved. You can view your progress on your profile page anytime.
    Can I suggest a topic for a new lesson?
    Absolutely! We love hearing from learners. Use the contact form above and select "Content Suggestion" — we read every message.
    Will I get a certificate after finishing?
    Currently we don't issue certificates, but we're working on a system to recognize learners who complete full learning paths. Stay tuned!
